About

Built by people who get audited for a living.

Sentinel Assurance Group was founded by the operators of Arcanna Analytics, an ISO/IEC 17025-accredited, DEA-registered analytical testing laboratory.

Years of operating under annual accreditation assessments — method validation, measurement uncertainty, impartiality controls, corrective-action discipline — left us with a conviction: AI needed the same third-party assurance infrastructure that every other high-stakes industry already has.

The accreditation world agrees. ANAB — the ANSI National Accreditation Board that accredits laboratories like ours — is the same body now accrediting ISO/IEC 42001 certification programs. We come from inside that ecosystem. Most AI consultants are discovering it on Wikipedia.

We serve regulated and reputation-sensitive businesses: healthcare, financial services, employers deploying AI in hiring and scheduling, and software companies whose customers are starting to ask hard questions.

Impartiality statement

We do not sell or resell AI products, take referral fees from vendors we assess, or issue certifications. Assessment and advisory are separated in every engagement where independence matters.
